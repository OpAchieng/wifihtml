About Swift Fibre Solutions

Swift Fibre Solutions Ltd is a trusted Internet Service Provider (ISP) delivering fast, secure, and reliable high-speed internet. We partner with real estate agents, property managers, and tenants to ensure buildings stay connected with seamless fibre and wireless broadband solutions.

Our Mission

To provide dependable, high-speed internet access that enhances tenant satisfaction, supported by 24/7 technical assistance and robust security measures for safe online experiences.

Our Vision

To be the preferred internet partner for real estate agents, property managers, and tenants by delivering reliable connectivity and unmatched customer support across Kenya and beyond.

Our Goals

  • Provide secure, high-speed internet to tenants in properties managed by our partners.
  • Offer a revenue-sharing model for real estate agents to earn ongoing commissions.
  • Enhance tenant satisfaction and property appeal with reliable, high-quality services.
  • Build long-term partnerships that benefit agents, tenants, and property owners.

Implementation Plan

  • Agreement Signing: Once partnership terms are agreed upon, a formal agreement is signed.
  • Service Setup for Tenants: We collaborate with your team to provide simple installation and setup options for tenants.
  • Agent Onboarding: Agents receive training on how to market the service and tools to track their commissions.
  • Revenue Tracking: Agents access an online portal to monitor subscriptions, commissions earned, and performance incentives.

Why Choose Us

  • ✔ Proven reliability with minimal downtime
  • ✔ Scalable internet solutions for any building size
  • ✔ Easy integration with minimal disruption
  • ✔ Long-term partnerships with marketing and technical support
